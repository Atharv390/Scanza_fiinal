About Scanza

Scanza is a modern solution for restaurants to provide digital menus through QR codes. It helps reduce paper waste, offers easy updates, and enhances the customer experience.

How to Use Scanza

  1. Scan the QR code placed on the table using your smartphone camera.

  2. Open the link that appears on your screen.

  3. Browse the digital menu and place your order with ease.

Benefits of Using Scanza

  1. Reduces paper waste and is eco-friendly.

  2. Instant updates to the menu without reprinting costs.

  3. Enhances customer experience with interactive menus.

  4. Improves hygiene by minimizing physical contact.

  5. Provides detailed dish descriptions and images.
